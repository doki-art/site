— Approach

How I work.

/ 01

Anyone with AI can generate fifty options. I know which one ships.

Generation got cheap. Judgment didn't. Thirteen years of complex B2B is what filters the fifty down to one.

/ 02

A stuck problem usually just means the wrong room.

The hardest work isn't features that exist — it's the ones with no precedent. I bring product, engineering, and real users to the table, and we solve it there.

/ 03

I see it through — discovery to handoff, no gaps.

Discovery, system, prototype, handoff — then back through it when the build pushes back. I move fast where speed is cheap and slow down where judgment isn't. The process bends to the problem; it isn't a fixed set of steps.
